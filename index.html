Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection, iptionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables, or meats.

Preperation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preperation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheeses, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over meduim heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pou in the eggs, Tilt the pan to ensure evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings oven one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the finllings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional slat and pepeer if needed.
